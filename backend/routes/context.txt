Panda Express Menu and General Information

GENERAL INFORMATION:
- Founded: 1983 in Glendale, California.
- Founders: Andrew Cherng, Peggy Cherng, and Master Chef Ming-Tsai Cherng.
- Headquarters: Rosemead, California, U.S.
- Locations: Over 2,400 worldwide as of 2022.
- Cuisine: American Chinese, with popular dishes like Orange Chicken and Beijing Beef.

ALLERGEN INFORMATION:
Panda Express uses ingredients that contain all major FDA allergens: peanuts, tree nuts, eggs, fish, shellfish, milk, soy, wheat, and sesame.
Cross-contact between ingredients is possible. For detailed allergen information, refer to their official website.
